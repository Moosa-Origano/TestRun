What is the Cosmere?

The Cosmere is an expansive fantasy universe that connects multiple series and worlds, each with its own unique magic systems, cultures, and characters. Created by author Brandon Sanderson, these worlds are part of a grander narrative, with connections and secrets waiting to be discovered across series like Mistborn, The Stormlight Archive, and Elantris.

Cosmere Worlds

Scadrial

Home to the Mistborn series, Scadrial is a world where magic is fueled by metals and where a dark lord once ruled with an iron fist.

Roshar

The setting of The Stormlight Archive, Roshar is a storm-blasted world where knights wield mystical blades and powers to protect humanity from ancient forces.

Sel

Known from Elantris and The Emperor's Soul, Sel is a world of rune-based magic and political intrigue, where the fallen city of Elantris once stood as a beacon of light.

Taldain

In White Sand, Taldain is a desert world where magic is drawn from sand, influenced by the planet's two distinct suns.

Discover the Connections

The Cosmere is woven together through hints, crossovers, and mysteries that enrich each series. Follow characters like Hoid, a mysterious traveler who appears across worlds, or delve into the lore of the Shards, powerful entities that shape each realm.

Whether you're just starting with Mistborn or already deep into The Stormlight Archive, every book is a doorway to the grand tapestry of the Cosmere.
